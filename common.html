The youngest girl to complete a motorcycle journey across Pakistan and Ladakh is Harini. She embarked on this adventurous journey at the age of 18 in 2024.

Harini expedition was notable not only for her youth but also for challenging societal norms about women and motorcycling in India. Her journey was documented and inspired many, highlighting her courage and determination to explore the world on two wheels.

Her smile mirrors the bike's shine, and together they create a scene of harmonious allure, where the beauty of craftsmanship meets the charm of a captivating soul.
